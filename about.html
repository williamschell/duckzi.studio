Duckzi

Game Developer, Artist

Hello! I am William Schell (or duckzi). I have been creating games since I was in 5th grade, and have remained dedicated to the craft ever since. I have spent the last few years developing my universe in which all of my games take place in, and am now beginning to release games in the franchise. Over the many years I have spent working on games for this much larger project, I have taught myself much in digital art, programming, modeling, animation and rigging, music and much more. There's still much for me to learn though and I strive for more excellence every day. If you're reading this, thank you for checking me out and try out my newest game!
